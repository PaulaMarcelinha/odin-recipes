Lemon Loaf

Description

Easy, lemony and moist, lemon drizzle cake

Ingredients

Cake
  • 250g unsalted butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 250g self-raising flour
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 lemons
Drizzle
  • 75ml lemon juice
  • 75g sugar
Decoration
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • lemon zest

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160C. Grease a loaf tin.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until light and creamy.
  3. Add in flour, eggs and lemon, whisk until smooth.
  4. Pour the cake mixture into the tin. Bake for 50-60 minutes,
  5. Meanwhile, mix lemon juice and caster sugar.
  6. Drizzle the mixture on the cake once cooled.
  7. Remove the cake from the tin. Mix lemon and icing sugar together and pour over the loaf.
  8. Sprinkle some lemon zest over the loaf, if desired.
