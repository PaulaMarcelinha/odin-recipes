The Ultimate Brownies

Description

Simple yet fudgy brownies. These might be the best you have ever tried.

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 250g sugar
  • 135g plain flour
  • pinch of salt
  • optional: 50-100g of dark chocolate for sprinkling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven 160Â°C
  2. Cut butter up into small pieces and place in a saucepan on small fire.
  3. Break chocolate into smaller pieces and place together with the butter until melted. Remove and let it cool down.
  4. In a separate bowl whisk eggs together with sugar. Add cooled and melted chocolate with butter. Mix until smooth.
  5. Add plain flour and a pinch of salt, whisk together.
  6. Pour into a pan, around 25cm x 25cm.
  7. Optional: Grate chocolate or cut up into smaller pieces and place all over the brownie mixture before baking
  8. Bake for around 25-30 minutes.
  9. Once baked and cooled, cut into desired size
