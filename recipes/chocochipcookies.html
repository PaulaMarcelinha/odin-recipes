Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 280g self-raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 170g unsalted butter
  • 150g light or dark brown sugar
  • 100g granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 225g chocolate chips or chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk flour, baking soda and salt together.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk melted butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar together, until no lumps remain.
  3. Whisk in the egg in the medium bowl.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into wet ingredients and mix together. Then fold in the chocolate chips or chocolate chunks.
  5. Cover the dough tightly and place in the fridge for at least 2-3 hours or overnight to chill.
  6. Take a dough out and allow it to soften slightly for 10 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile preheat the oven to 165Â°C
  8. Using a cookie scoop or tablespoon, scoop out dough and roll into balls, then place on the parchment paper.
  9. Bake cookies for 12-13 minutes.
